# IT Hardware Lifecycle & Technical Standards

## 1. Standard Issue Models (2024-2025)
### Tier A (Engineering/Design)
- **Model**: MacBook Pro 14" (M3 Pro, 36GB RAM, 1TB SSD).
- **Warranty**: AppleCare+ for Enterprise (4 Years).
### Tier B (General Business)
- **Model**: MacBook Air 13" (M3, 16GB RAM, 512GB SSD) OR Dell Latitude 5440 (Core i7, 16GB, 512GB).

## 2. Repair Process (Deep Dive)
### Cracked Screens
- **Policy**: 1 accidental damage event covered per year. Subsequent damages require department budget approval.
- **Process**: Submit ticket -> Receive loaner -> Device sent to Depot -> 3-5 day turnaround.
### Battery Replacement
- **Threshold**: Replaced if "Maximum Capacity" in System Settings is below 80% or "Cycle Count" exceeds 1000.

## 3. Peripheral Compatibility Matrix
- **Monitors**: Standard is Dell U2723QE (4K, USB-C Charging).
- **Docks**: Logitech Logi Dock or Dell WD22TB4. 
- **Cables**: Thunderbolt 4 cables (marked with a lightning bolt and '4') are REQUIRED for dual-monitor setups via dock. Standard USB-C charging cables will NOT work for video.

## 4. End-of-Life (EOL) Procedure
- After 48 months, devices are eligible for "Buy-Back" or "Donation".
- Data must be wiped via Jamf "Erase Device" (Mac) or Intune "Wipe" (Windows) before physical return.
