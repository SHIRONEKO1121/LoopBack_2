# Hardware Procurement & Laptop Request Policy

## Eligibility
- **New Employees**: A standard-issue laptop (MacBook Air or ThinkPad T-series) is provided on Day 1.
- **Upgrades**: Employees are eligible for a hardware refresh every 3 years.
- **Repairs**: If your equipment is damaged, a loaner will be provided during repair.

## Requesting New Hardware
1. **Approval**: Obtain email approval from your direct manager.
2. **Submission**: Open the 'IT Catalog' on the intranet and select the desired model.
3. **Form**: Complete Form IT-09 (Procurement Request) and attach the manager approval.
4. **Timing**: Requests take 5-7 business days to process once approved.

## Specialized Equipment
- Requests for GPUs, high-RAM workstations, or dual monitors require Department Head approval.
- Accessories (mice, keyboards, adapters) under $50 can be requested directly from the IT supplies cabinet on Floor 2.
